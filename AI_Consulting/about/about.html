About the Consultant

I am a recognized professor and subject matter expert in Artificial Intelligence, dedicated to providing educational consulting services that help organizations understand, evaluate, and strategically approach AI adoption. My focus is on knowledge transfer, best practices, and ethical considerations rather than hands-on implementation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of consulting services do you provide?

I provide educational and advisory consulting services focused on AI knowledge transfer, strategy development, ethical considerations, and best practices. This is informational consulting rather than hands-on implementation or solution building.

What industries do you work with?

I work with organizations across various industries seeking to understand AI technologies, develop AI strategies, and navigate ethical and regulatory considerations. My academic background provides broad applicability across sectors.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Engagements typically include: 1) Educational workshops and training sessions, 2) Strategic advisory on AI adoption, 3) Expert guidance on AI ethics and governance, 4) Knowledge transfer sessions, and 5) Ongoing consultation on AI-related questions and challenges.

Credentials & Expertise

  • Recognized professor in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Subject matter expert with extensive research and academic publications
  • Deep expertise in AI ethics, responsible AI, and governance frameworks
  • Experience educating professionals and organizations on AI technologies
  • Academic background in computer science, AI, and related disciplines
